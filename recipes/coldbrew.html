Cold Brew Coffee

Cold-brewed coffee has become a coffeehouse hit, but those prices aren't.

Cold-brewed coffee sales at trendy coffeehouses have shot up in the last few years, and they show no signs of cooling off. Yet making cold brew coffee is as basic as its name implies: coffee brewed without heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coffee beans, course ground
  • 4 cups fresh water

Directions

  1. Put beans into a container.
  2. Pour in water
  3. Stir
  4. Let sit for 24 hours.
  5. Filter out grounds
